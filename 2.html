COOKIE MONSTER

Cookie Monster is a Muppet on the long-running children's television show Sesame Street.
He is best known for his voracious appetite and his famous eating phrases,

"Me want cookie!"

"Me eat cookie!"

"Om nom nom nom"

He eats almost anything, including normally inedible objects.
